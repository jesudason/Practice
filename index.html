CNY Salad Recipe (ordered)

  1. Deseed Pomegranate
    • Cut the top and bottom of the pomegranante off
    • Score the pomegranate along each section
    • Using a wooden spoon, tap the skin of the pomegranate into a bowl filled with water until all the seeds have fallen out
    • Skim the small pieces of white pith off the top of the bowl of water, strain the pomegranate seeds and set aside
  2. Cook pearl barley according to the instructions on the packet. Run cooked grains under cold water to cool quickly, strain.
  3. Chop mint, dill and parsley and set aside
  4. In a small bowl, mix lemon juice, olive oil, salt, pepper & honey together and place in refrigerator for 30 minutes while the flavours meld
  5. Chop apples into small bite-sized pieces and mix well with the salad dressing
  6. In a large bowl, combine the cooked pearl barley, pomegranate, herbs and salad dressing.
